The one thing that's off-limits: **repackaging and selling React Bytes itself.**

That means you may not take the components (as-is or lightly modified) and:

- Sell them as your own component library, UI kit, or template pack

If what you're selling is your own app, website, or tool that happens to *use* React Bytes components under the hood — that's fine. If what you're selling is the components themselves — that's not.
